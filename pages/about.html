About Touch Learning Center

Touch Learning Center is a refugee learning center located in Pandan Cahaya, Ampang, Selangor, Malaysia founded since 2006. Our students are of different nationalities, religions and tribes primarily refugee from Myanmar. We believe in a society where we can all live together in harmony regardless of how different our ideas are, how significant our beliefs are or how distant our cultures might be. Let's be the light of the world! Join us in fulfilling the wonderful dreams of our children together at TLC!
